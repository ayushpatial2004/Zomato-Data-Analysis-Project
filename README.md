# 🍽️ Zomato Data Analysis Project

## ⭐ SITUATION

Analyzed a dataset of **19,645 Zomato restaurant records** to understand customer behavior and restaurant performance patterns.

---

## 🎯 TASK

Identify key factors driving customer engagement and uncover actionable business insights.

---

## ⚙️ ACTION

* Cleaned and processed raw dataset
* Converted ratings and cost into usable numeric format
* Performed multi-factor analysis on:

  * Location
  * Ratings
  * Pricing
  * Online availability
  * Table booking

---

## 📈 RESULTS (KEY INSIGHTS)

* 📍 **Location Impact**
  Certain locations consistently show higher customer engagement and retention.

* ⭐ **Ratings Drive Customers**
  Restaurants with higher ratings attract significantly more customer interaction (votes).

* 🌐 **Online vs Offline**
  Online availability does not always translate into higher engagement — offline presence still matters.

* 💰 **Pricing Influence**
  Moderate pricing tends to perform better than extremely high or low pricing.

* 💎 **Hidden Gems Identified**
  Found multiple restaurants with high ratings and affordable pricing but low visibility, indicating untapped potential.

* 🪑 **Table Booking Impact**
  Restaurants offering table booking show slightly better customer satisfaction, but impact is limited.

---

## 🧠 CONCLUSION

Customer engagement is primarily driven by **ratings, location, and pricing**, while features like online ordering and table booking play a secondary role.
